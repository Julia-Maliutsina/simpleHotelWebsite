Our tasting focus on:

  • Reds – Cabernet Sauvignon, Pinot Noir, Zinfandel,
  • Whites – Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, RieslingSummer Selections – Rosé, Prosecco
  • Regional Specific – Italian Wines, South American Wines, California Reds
  • Specific Varietals (Grapes)– Cabernet Francs, Nebbiolos, Carignans
  • Styles – Full Bodied Reds, Zesty Whites, Sparkling, Desert Wines.

Our wine tastings, Wine Moments, are held in the beautiful Wintergarden Restaurant and Bar. The tastings are suitable for everyone over age 18, and no absolutely prior knowledge about wine is needed. Pick your favorite Wine Moment or experience them all! Natural Wine Momentoffers tasting low intervention and conventional wines side by side, understanding the difference and the flavours. Sparkling Wine Moment is a tasting of sparkling wines made in different methods and countries. Chardonnay Moment allows you to try wines made from Chardonnay grape coming from different parts of the world. Custom-made wine tasting is a tasting is made specifically according to your wishes on any topic (such as particular grape, country, region, style, winemaker).
